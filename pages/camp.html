Rebel Makers Camp is an annual celebration of digital making for everyone.

Rebel Makers Camp is a one day festival comprising workshops, speakers, and the ability to work on your own projects with friends.

Our aim is to bring together people of all ages and abilities to share skills, discover technology and socialise in a range of fun and engaging ways.
